Nearby Airports

If you are planning on taking a flight, the following three airports are the ones nearer to the island. All of this airports have shuttles that can take you to the Island and there is a free transportation bus system once you get to South Padre Island. Rental cars and taxis are available as well.

Valley International Airport (HRL)

City: Harlingen, TX
Driving distance: 46 miles or 74 km
Address: 3002 Heritage Way, Harlingen, TX 78550

McAllen-Miller International Airport (MFE)

City: McAllen, TX
Driving distance: 80 miles or 128 km
Address: 2500 S Bicentennial Blvd, McAllen, TX 78503

Brownsville/South Padre Island International Airport (BRO)

City: Brownsville, TX
Driving distance: 27 miles or 43 km
Address: 700 Amelia Earhart Dr, Brownsville, TX 78521
